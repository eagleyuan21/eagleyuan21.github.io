EAGLE'S Minesweeper

This game includes a few features such as setting the dimensions and total mines. The dimensions are kept between 15 and 35, and the number of mines cannot exceed the total number of cells. There is a timer feature, which turns red when you lose and green when you win. It will start on the click of the first cell. To win, you can either flag every single mine and not flag any other cells, or unhide every non mine cell. Left click is unhiding a cell, and right click is flagging a cell. Use right click to unflag a flagged cell.
